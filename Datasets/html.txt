<h2 id="mp-tfa-h2" class="mp-h2 mw-html-heading">From today's featured article</h2>
<div id="mp-tfa" class="mp-contains-float"><div id="mp-tfa-img" style="float: left; margin: 0.5em 0.9em 0.4em 0em;">
<div class="thumbinner mp-thumb" style="background: transparent; color: inherit; border: none; padding: 0; max-width: 160px;">
<span typeof="mw:File"><a href="/wiki/File:Boat_Race_2018_-_5_team_members_Women%27s_Reserve_Race_(06).jpg" class="mw-file-description" title="Members of the victorious Blondie crew"><img alt="Members of the victorious Blondie crew" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0b/Boat_Race_2018_-_5_team_members_Women%27s_Reserve_Race_%2806%29.jpg/250px-Boat_Race_2018_-_5_team_members_Women%27s_Reserve_Race_%2806%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="160" height="107" class="mw-file-element" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0b/Boat_Race_2018_-_5_team_members_Women%27s_Reserve_Race_%2806%29.jpg/330px-Boat_Race_2018_-_5_team_members_Women%27s_Reserve_Race_%2806%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4320" data-file-height="2880" /></a></span><div class="thumbcaption" style="padding: 0.25em 0; word-wrap: break-word;">Members of the victorious <i>Blondie</i> crew</div></div>

<p><b><a href="/wiki/The_Boat_Race_2018" title="The Boat Race 2018">The Boat Race 2018</a></b> took place on 24&#160;March. Held annually, <a href="/wiki/The_Boat_Race" title="The Boat Race">The Boat Race</a> is a <a href="/wiki/Rowing_(sport)#Side_by_side" title="Rowing (sport)">side-by-side rowing</a> race between crews from the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Oxford" title="University of Oxford">universities of Oxford</a> and <a href="/wiki/University_of_Cambridge" title="University of Cambridge">Cambridge</a> along a 4.2-mile (6.8&#160;km) tidal stretch of the <a href="/wiki/River_Thames" title="River Thames">River Thames</a> in south-west London, England. For the third time in the history of the event, the men's, the <a href="/wiki/Women%27s_Boat_Race" title="Women&#39;s Boat Race">women's</a> and both reserves' races were all held on the <a href="/wiki/Tideway" title="Tideway">Tideway</a> on the same day. The women's race saw Cambridge lead from the start, eventually winning by a considerable margin to take the overall record to <span class="nowrap">43–30</span> in their favour. In the women's reserve race, Cambridge's <i>Blondie</i> <i>(crew pictured)</i> defeated Oxford's <i>Osiris</i> by nine lengths. The men's reserve race was won by Cambridge's <i>Goldie</i>, who defeated Oxford's <i>Isis</i> by a margin of four lengths. The men's race was the final event of the day and completed a <a href="/wiki/Whitewash_(sport)" title="Whitewash (sport)">whitewash</a> as Cambridge won, taking the overall record to <span class="nowrap">83–80</span> in their favour. The races were watched by around 250,000 spectators live, and broadcast around the world. (<b><a href="/wiki/The_Boat_Race_2018" title="The Boat Race 2018">Full&#160;article...</a></b>)
</p>
